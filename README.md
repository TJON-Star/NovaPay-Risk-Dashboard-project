NovaPay Risk Dashboard

📖 Overview
The NovaPay Risk Dashboard is a professional tool for monitoring compliance and risk metrics.  
It helps organizations visualize key risk indicators, streamline reporting, and support governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) decision-making.  

NovaPay is a fictional Nigerian fintech company processing card payments. This assessment covers 20 information security risks across ISO 27001:2022, PCI-DSS v4.0, and NDPR compliance domains. Four critical risks were identified requiring immediate remediation, with 10 risks currently open and assigned to control owners.
